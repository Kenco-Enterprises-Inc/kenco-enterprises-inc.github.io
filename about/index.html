About Kenco

Our History

Kenco was founded in January of 1973 and incorporated in 1974 as Kenco Enterprises, Inc. In 1975 the first dedicated Monitoring Service in the state was initiated at Kenco. Kenco has the has the only Monitoring Center of its kind in its service area (Montana and Wyoming) that is designed, built and staffed solely for alarm monitoring purposes.

Today the corporation is owned by Kenneth R. Brown (CEO), Mark A. Chaput (CFO) and Dirk Bauwens (President and COO).

Kenneth R. Brown
Ken had a strong desire to own his own company and to treat customers the way he felt they should be treated. With his wife Karen, together they founded Kenco in January of 1973. With Navy electronics training and 10 years experience as an IBM service technician, Ken started Kenco Security and Technology and has remained CEO of the corporation since its inception.
Mark A. Chaput
Mark joined Kenco as a technician in 1979 with an Associate Degree in Electrical Technology and 2 years experience with an electrical engineering firm.
Dirk Bauwens
Dirk has been with the company since 1979. He received his electrical engineering education from MSU Bozeman, prior to joining Kenco as the Service and Operations Manager.

Our Philosophy

We have a simple business philosophy of treating our customers the way we ourselves want to be treated. With this philosophy we have thousands of satisfied customers. Anytime you have a question, concern or problem, give us a call. We're here for you 24/7!
